ART ABOUT CONTACT

Transactions

The Transactions exhibition was held at Buffalo Arts Studio, April 7th - May 26th 2012. The exhibition included six large format digital prints (Bow, Cartwheels, Catch, Fight, Handshake, and Swing Yer Partner), a laser-cut acrylic sculpture (Embrace), the two Sculptures With Time, and a video projection which alternated between the HD Video Dynamism of a Dog in a Wheelchair and the interactive piece Chronoscape.

Chronoscape

Chronoscape is a live interactive version of the Transactions project and based on the same technology used in Dynamism of a Dog in a Wheelchair. This installation was developed as part of my MFA Thesis exhibition.

Time Dissection

Time Dissection is a collection of experiments in alternate perceptions of time taking the form of reactive video projection and sculptural objects. Using custom software and a high speed camera, Time Dissection conflates the temporal and spatial dimensions of the subject.
